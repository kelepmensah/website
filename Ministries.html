Let's Forgive one another

Epesians 4:32 says, "Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ, God forgave you.

How do you feel after you've been hurt by someone really close to you? It is very difficult to let memories like that out of your head. Sometime we seek revenge, wjich always makes it worse.

Our Bible reading says we should forgive one another. Not only to forgive, but to be KIND and COMPASIONATE towards them. Who has offended you? Find a place in your heart to forgive that person.
